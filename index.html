HOD's Message


Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering at Don Bosco Institute of Technology! At the heart of our department lies a steadfast commitment to academic excellence, innovative thinking, and holistic development. Our vision is to become a center of academic distinction in the field of computer science and engineering, fostering technically proficient professionals who contribute meaningfully to society.

In alignment with this vision, we continuously strive to develop an academic ecosystem that prioritizes an effective and engaging teaching-learning process. Our dedicated faculty, enthusiastic students, and supportive stakeholders collaboratively ensure a culture of consistent improvement through evaluation, feedback, and participation.

We are proud to actively encourage undergraduate research in emerging domains of computer science. By providing a dynamic learning environment, we enable our students to sharpen their problem-solving abilities while upholding professional ethics and social responsibilities.

As the Head of the Department, I extend my gratitude to all stakeholders – our students, faculty, industry partners, and alumni – for their unwavering support in achieving our shared goals. Together, we continue to make strides toward innovation, excellence, and service to society.

I welcome you to explore the opportunities within our department and join us in our journey of learning, discovery, and growth. Warm regards,

Ms. Dipti Jadhav
Head of Department, Computer Engineering
